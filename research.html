Our AI research focuses on developing intelligent systems for healthcare, agriculture, education, and automation. We work on machine learning, deep learning, natural language processing, and computer vision applications tailored to African contexts.

We conduct research on securing digital infrastructure, developing encryption protocols, threat detection systems, and privacy-preserving technologies. Our work addresses the unique cybersecurity challenges faced in developing regions.
