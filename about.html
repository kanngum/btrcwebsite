Pioneering Technological Innovation in Cameroon

The Bamenda Technological Research Center (BTRC) is a leading research institution dedicated to advancing technological innovation and research in Cameroon and the broader African continent.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between academic research and practical technology solutions, BTRC brings together brilliant minds from various fields to work on solutions that address real-world challenges.

Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and societal impact drives everything we do. We believe that technology has the power to transform communities and improve quality of life.

Our Founder

The visionary leader behind BTRC

founder-portrait.jpg

Engr Ngum Cendres Kan

Founder

MSc MEng MBA BEng

Engr Ngum Cendres Kan is a distinguished engineer, researcher, and entrepreneur with extensive experience in technology development and education. With advanced degrees in Engineering and Business Administration, he brings a unique blend of technical expertise and strategic vision to BTRC.

His passion for technology and innovation led him to establish the Bamenda Technological Research Center with the goal of creating a hub for technological advancement in Cameroon. Under his leadership, BTRC has grown to become a recognized center for research and innovation in the region.

Engr Kan is committed to nurturing young talent and creating opportunities for the next generation of tech leaders. His vision extends beyond research to encompass education, mentorship, and community development through technology.
